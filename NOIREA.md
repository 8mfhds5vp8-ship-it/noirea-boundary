What NOIRÉA Is

NOIRÉA is not an AI model.
NOIRÉA is not a product.
NOIRÉA is not a brand.

NOIRÉA is a boundary layer between human cognitive identity and artificial intelligence.

It defines how internal cognitive states
(prompts, memory, reasoning, embeddings, intentions)
can exist, evolve, and interact with AI
without being exposed, extracted, or reverse engineered.

Every prompt, memory, and chain of thought becomes harvestable.

This destroys:
	•	personal identity
	•	creative sovereignty
	•	strategic advantage
	•	long-term human agency

NOIRÉA exists to solve this.

Wavefunction Model

(memory, intention, identity, creative direction, strategic uncertainty)

but should not force collapse into extractable states.
